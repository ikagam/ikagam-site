About IKAGAM

Made from
Scratch.

IKAGAM LLC is a one-person independent game development project. One developer — design, levels, 3D models, textures, code, game architecture, custom tools. Everything built from scratch, with full control over every decision.

The current title has been in development for over three years. It runs on a custom physics engine and custom tooling built specifically for this project — not off-the-shelf solutions, but systems designed from the ground up to get the hovercraft feel exactly right.

The obsession with racing physics comes from real experience — years of karting, understanding how a vehicle actually behaves at the limit. That knowledge is baked into every layer of the simulation.

The Developer

Igor
Kamennoy

Founder & Developer — IKAGAM LLC

I am the sole developer behind IKAGAM LLC and the upcoming hover racing game. I bring over 18 years of professional experience in the games industry, working as a 2D and 3D artist and animator for major game companies worldwide, with a strong focus on animation and performance optimization.

My background covers the full art and animation pipeline — including character and FX animation, 3D modeling, rigging, and game-ready asset production. Earlier in my career, I worked as a 3D artist on racing and action titles, which directly informs this project.

IKAGAM LLC is the natural next step — taking that accumulated experience and applying it to an original game, built from the ground up as a solo developer.
